About

Musician turned software engineer. I decided to take the plunge and change my career as I wanted something a bit more fulfilling and challenging. I’ve also always been interested in technology so decided I would take it one step further and enrol in a software development bootcamp.

Problem-solving is something I have always been great at, whether it be working with difficult people or assembling Ikea flatpack furniture, I have always found ways through complicated situations.

A hard work ethic is something I believe everyone should have, as it is a positive characteristic that seeps into not just your professional life, but also your personal. By my own reckoning, this is something I have cultivated through years of working demanding jobs to the utmost of my abilities.
